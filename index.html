How it started

A glass tipped over in the grass. That was sort of it.

Arnaud grew up around French tables where things were always placed with a little care. He moved to Gothenburg, kept picnicking the way he always had — and kept losing wine to uneven ground. SOKL started as a fix for one evening and became the first thing Atelier Båge makes.

Stays put

Stakes into grass, sand, or soft ground. Holds your stems at a steady tilt so nothing spills, even on a slope.

Folds to pocket size

Collapses flat for the walk there. No bag too small, no picnic too spontaneous.

Hand-finished, every one

3D-printed in Gothenburg, then sanded and finished by hand. Small batches — never more than the next one needs to be.
